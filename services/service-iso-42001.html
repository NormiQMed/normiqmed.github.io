ISO 42001 Integration for Medical Devices

What This Service Covers

  • Integration of ISO/IEC 42001 AI management system within medical device QMS

  • Definition of AI-specific policies, controls, and accountability structures
  • Risk and performance monitoring aligned to AI lifecycle requirements
  • Harmonization of AI management with regulatory expectations

When This Service Is Typically Required

  • Organizations implementing AI management systems alongside medical device QMS
  • Expansion into AI-enabled products requiring structured governance
  • Alignment of AI practices with emerging regulatory and standard expectations
  • Integration of standalone AI controls into a regulated QMS environment

Applicable Regulations & Standards

  • ISO/IEC 42001
  • ISO 13485
  • ISO 14971
  • IEC 62304
  • Applicable AI regulatory guidance (FDA, EU)

How This Fits Within the Quality System

ISO 42001 controls are integrated into the QMS structure, ensuring AI governance operates within existing quality system processes rather than as a parallel management system.
